The Board

Neil Sangra

I am a high school senior in North Texas with a passion for volunteering and technology. I enjoy tutoring fellow students and exploring computer science. Visiting my grandparents in India has taught me the value of spending time with elders, and I want to make a positive impact in this area. Inspired by bringing joy to my own grandparents, I collaborated with friends to create Givers of Tomorrow. My goal is to improve the lives of senior citizens in my community through this initiative.

Preetham Manapuri

I am a senior at Allen High School in Texas, and I have a strong desire to help others using technology. I am grateful for the opportunity to make a positive impact in my community through my technological skills, and I aspire to solve more problems through computer science. It saddens me that many avoidable problems persist because of a lack of care for patients. Losing one's memory can be devastating, and I aim to prevent such suffering among the elderly in nursing homes. Alongside my team, I hope to have a positive influence on the lives of these individuals.

Azariah Teklu

I am a current high school senior in North Texas. My interest in medicine leads me to read medical textbooks in my free time. Once, I happened upon a page about Alzheimer's disease and how it prominently affects seniors. I wanted to strengthen efforts to lower the growth of this disease and I realized that not all nursing homes have the resources to improve memory, so I want to provide a volunteering service and memory improvement games to aid them.


Aarav Sharma

Aarav, a senior at Allen High School, is determined to aid those with memory conditions like Alzheimer's and dementia. Through Givers of Tomorrow, he extends his impact beyond nursing home volunteering. With a passion for robotics and technology, Aarav plans to pursue engineering in college. His fascination with Legos from an early age sparked his interest in robotics, and now he aims to merge his technical expertise with humanitarian efforts. Aarav's ultimate goal is to create a meaningful and lasting impact in his community.

Sahil Pai

As a senior at Allen High School in Texas, I'm passionate about using technology to create convenience and simplicity in daily life. Combining this interest with my love for volunteering, I've raised money for charities through a guitar band. Inspired by my mother's work teaching yoga to senior citizens, I aim to make a positive impact in the elderly community of North Texas. With my technological skills and volunteering experiences, I'm dedicated to improving the lives of elderly individuals through various means.
